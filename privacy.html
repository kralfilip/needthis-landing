Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

1. Introduction

NeedThat ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our service.

2. Information We Collect

When you authorize NeedThat to post on your TikTok account, we collect:

  • TikTok Account Information: Username, profile ID, and basic profile information
  • Authorization Token: Access token to post videos on your behalf
  • Usage Data: Information about videos posted, timestamps, and posting status

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Post product discovery videos to your TikTok account as authorized
  • Monitor posting status and performance
  • Comply with legal requirements

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • TikTok: Required to post videos via their API
  • Service Providers: Cloud hosting and infrastructure providers
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law

5. TikTok Integration

Our service integrates with TikTok's Content Posting API. By authorizing our app:

  • You grant us permission to post videos on your behalf
  • You can revoke access at any time through TikTok's settings
  • We comply with TikTok's Terms of Service and API guidelines

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure storage of access tokens
  • Regular security audits

7. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide our service. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Revoke authorization at any time
  • Object to processing of your data

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on this page.

11. Contact Us

For questions about this Privacy Policy or to exercise your rights, contact us at:

Email: privacy@needthat.app
